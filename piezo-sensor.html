Piezo Impact Sensing

Arduino · Piezo sensor · FFT / SPL · Web Serial

A low-cost piezo-disc impact sensor on an Arduino Uno for capturing the strike that excites an instrument, with a browser-based Web Serial visualiser for the impulse and its spectrum.

Circuit

27 mm piezo disc, 1 MΩ resistor across it, clamping diodes on A0 to protect the analog input. Fill in detail and a wiring figure.
